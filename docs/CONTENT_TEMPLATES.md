## CONTENT WRITING TIPS

### Voice & Tone Consistency
- Match professional, confident tone of Intentional Interiors
- Balance expertise with approachability
- Focus on client benefits
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences clear and concise

### Key Phrases
- "Across the UAE and beyond"
- "Established Business/Division"
- "Years of experience"
- "Proven track record"
- "Expert/Experienced team"

### Content Length Guidelines
- About section: 150-200 words
- Each service: 30-50 words
- Why Choose Us: 10-20 words per point
- Process steps: 10-20 words per step
- Projects: 50-100 words
- Total page: ~500-700 words

### SEO Considerations
- Include relevant keywords naturally
- Use location terms (UAE, Dubai)
- Include industry terminology
- Mention target audiences
- Action words in CTAs
