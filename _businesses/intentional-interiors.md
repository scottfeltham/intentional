## About Intentional Interiors

Intentional Interiors is our established division specializing in comprehensive fitout solutions for all internal spaces across the UAE and beyond. With years of experience and a proven track record, we transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary environments that inspire relaxation, productivity, creativity, and learning.

## Our Services

### F&B (Food & Beverage)
We achieve your desired ambience while at the same time creating, with you, functional F&B areas including back of house kitchens with their optimised equipment.

### Hotels
Time is money as they say, especially when it comes to opening hotels. We fast track beautiful designs and execute rooms and public spaces. We can also handle the back of house space with the same dedication.

### Commercial Offices
We create modern, functional workspaces that reflect your company's culture and support your team's success. From open-plan offices to executive suites, we handle every detail with precision and care. The "Work from Home in the Office" is our speciality.

### Residencies
Home is where the heart is. We work with you to create incredible family rest and relaxation spaces whether it is a single dwelling or an apartment complex.

### School & Educational Spaces
Our expertise in educational fitouts ensures that learning environments are safe, engaging, and conducive to student success. We understand the unique requirements of schools and deliver solutions that meet regulatory standards while inspiring young minds.

### Complete Project Management
From initial consultation to final handover, our experienced team manages every aspect of your project. We ensure on-time delivery, budget adherence, and exceptional quality throughout the entire process.

### Design & Build Services
Our integrated design and build approach streamlines the fitout process, ensuring seamless coordination between creative vision and practical execution. We have strategic relationships with multiple designers who create our concepts, and we complete the design down to working drawings.
